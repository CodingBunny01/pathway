Why This Matters

SDG 10 focuses on reducing inequalities. In education, inequality looks like unequal access to support, time, resources, and confidence.

Some students can afford tutors. Some have quiet homes to study in. Some do not.

Free and clear learning resources help close that gap. Education access builds confidence and future opportunity.

You Are Not Alone

Struggling does not mean you are lazy. Needing help does not mean you failed.

PATHWAY can help you understand topics and find free resources. If you need more support, these may help too.
