Find your own way forward.

Not everyone starts from the same place. Access to education, support, and opportunities isn’t equal. And so PATHWAY exists to help students find a way forward through free learning resources, guidance, and support.

A quieter place to learn.

PATHWAY is for students who feel overwhelmed, behind, or unsure where to begin. This is not about productivity or perfection. It is about learning at your own pace, without pressure.
