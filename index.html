Make your own way forward

Not everyone starts from the same place. Access to education, support, and opportunity is not equal, and so PATHWAY exists to help students find a way forward through free learning resources, guidance, and hope.

This is a space made for students who feel overwhelmed, behind, or unsure of where they stand. Learning is not a race. You are allowed to take your time.
