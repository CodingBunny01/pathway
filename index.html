Make your own way forward.

Not everyone starts from the same place. Access to education, support, and opportunities isn’t equal, and so PATHWAY exists to help students create their own way forward through free learning resources, guidance, and support.

A quieter place to learn.

This is a space for students who want to learn at their own pace, without pressure, comparison, or feeling left behind. You are allowed to take your time.
