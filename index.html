Find your way forward.

Not everyone starts from the same place. Access to education, support, and opportunity is not equal, and so PATHWAY exists to help students build their own way forward through free learning resources, guidance, and hope.

A quiet place to learn

PATHWAY is made for students who feel behind, overwhelmed, or unseen. This is not about perfection. It is about progress, curiosity, and believing that knowledge should be accessible to everyone.
