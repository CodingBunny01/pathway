Study Tips

Tip 1: Plan Your Study Time

Create a daily or weekly schedule that allocates specific time blocks for each subject. Structured time helps maintain focus and reduces procrastination.

Tip 2: Use Active Recall

Instead of just reading, quiz yourself on key concepts and try explaining them in your own words. This reinforces memory and understanding.

Tip 3: Take Breaks Effectively

Follow techniques like the Pomodoro method: study intensely for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. Breaks improve productivity and prevent burnout.
