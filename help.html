Study Tips

Consistency beats intensity

Studying regularly in small chunks helps retention more than cramming. Make a daily habit for best results.

Active recall & practice

Instead of just rereading notes, actively test yourself. Quizzes, flashcards, and teaching someone else work wonders.

Rest is productive

Breaks, sleep, and downtime improve learning. Overworking can reduce retention and focus.
