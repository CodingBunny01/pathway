Study Tips

Set Clear Goals

Break your study sessions into achievable objectives. This helps maintain focus and track your progress effectively.

Take Breaks

Regular breaks prevent burnout and improve information retention. Step away, stretch, or take a short walk to refresh your mind.

Use Active Learning

Engage with the material by summarizing, teaching others, or testing yourself. Active methods enhance understanding far better than passive reading.
