Sustainable Development Goal 10: Reduce Inequalities

Why SDG 10 Matters

Reducing inequalities is essential for creating a fair world where everyone has equal access to education, opportunities, and resources. Inequality limits potential, hampers growth, and creates social tension.

How SDG 10 Inspired Pathway

Seeing the gap in access to quality study resources motivated me to build Pathway. This platform ensures students from different backgrounds can access curated, high-quality learning materials in one place.

How This App Can Help

Pathway provides structured resources, relevant videos, and educational links for multiple subjects, helping students bridge learning gaps and empowering them to study efficiently, no matter their starting point.
