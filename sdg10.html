SDG 10 - Reducing Inequalities

Why SDG 10 Matters

Reducing inequalities is essential for creating a fair world where every individual has access to education, health care, and opportunities. When inequality persists, it limits personal potential, stifles societal growth, and can create social tension and unrest. By addressing inequality, we can empower communities, enhance economic stability, and ensure that everyone can contribute meaningfully to society.

How SDG 10 Inspired Pathway

Observing the disparities in educational resources and support, I felt motivated to create Pathway. Many students face barriers due to socioeconomic differences, and SDG 10 highlighted the importance of bridging these gaps. Pathway aims to provide a platform where quality learning materials are accessible to all, regardless of background, helping to reduce educational inequalities.

How This App Can Help

Pathway offers structured subject resources, curated videos, and helpful links to ensure students can learn efficiently. By presenting materials in an interactive and aesthetically engaging format, Pathway empowers students to study effectively and build confidence in their abilities, making education more inclusive and equitable.
