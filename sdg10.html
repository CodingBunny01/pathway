SDG 10 — Reducing Inequalities

Why SDG 10 Matters

SDG 10 focuses on reducing inequalities within and among societies — especially in access to education, opportunity, and resources. When inequality exists, talent is wasted, voices are silenced, and potential is lost. Education should never depend on privilege, location, or financial background.

A fair world is one where everyone starts with access — not advantage. Reducing inequality strengthens communities, promotes social stability, and allows individuals to reach their full potential.

How SDG 10 Inspired Pathway

While studying, I noticed how fragmented and unequal access to learning resources can be. Some students have endless support and guidance, while others struggle to even find where to start.

Pathway was created to address this gap — bringing structured, high-quality study materials into one accessible space. The goal is not to replace teachers, but to support students who need clarity, direction, and equal opportunity to learn.

How This App Can Help

Pathway provides curated subject resources, trusted educational videos, and guided study sections designed to reduce confusion and overwhelm. By centralizing learning materials, students can focus on understanding rather than searching.

This platform supports SDG 10 by making education more inclusive, empowering students regardless of background, and helping bridge learning gaps through accessibility and structure.
