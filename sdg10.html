Reducing Inequalities: SDG 10

Why SDG 10 Matters

Reducing inequalities is essential for creating a fair world where everyone has equal access to education, opportunities, and resources. Inequality limits potential, hampers growth, and creates social tension. Every student should have the tools to thrive, no matter their background.

How SDG 10 Inspired Pathway

Seeing the gap in access to quality study resources motivated me to build Pathway. This platform ensures students from different backgrounds can access curated, high-quality learning materials in one place. It drives the mission of equal opportunity in education.

How This App Can Help

Pathway provides structured resources, relevant videos, and educational links for multiple subjects. It helps students bridge learning gaps, empowers efficient studying, and ensures no one is left behind. The app promotes learning fairness while maintaining an interactive, engaging environment.
