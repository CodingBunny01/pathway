SDG 10 – Reduced Inequalities

Why it Matters

Inequalities limit opportunities for learning and growth. Many students do not have access to quality educational resources due to socio-economic factors, geographic location, or systemic barriers. Addressing these gaps is essential for fair development and empowerment of all students.

How SDG 10 Pushed Me

Learning about SDG 10 inspired me to create Pathway. I wanted to provide a platform where students, regardless of background, can access curated educational resources, videos, and study links. This aligns with the SDG’s mission of reducing disparities and promoting equal opportunity.

How Pathway Helps

Through curated subject sections, guided videos, study links, and interactive tools, Pathway provides students a way to access quality education at their fingertips. It’s designed to reduce the gap caused by unequal resources and empower students to achieve their learning goals.
